This is the main website and archive of Jakub Kris Rucinski. Rucinski is an author, entrepreneur, and visual artist. Some of Rucinski's most notable work is 'Paradigm Shift', an epic alternate-history thriller novel that is to date the longest book ever written and published by a person under 18 years of age. It has been recognized worldwide for its literary brilliance.

Jakub has also written the novella 'No Brakes' in collaboration with journalist Jamie Stangroom, and adapted several of his books, essays, and short stories into audiobook format. Jakub began the successful online firm 'Calico' which, up until its shutdown, edited video essays for over 20 YouTube channels and provided social media marketing services to hundreds through its dedicated panels, servers, and support system. Jakub Kris Rucinski's blog is available for archive, and the website also exists to provide a store link for all his products, ways to reach him, tools programmed or provided by Jakub, and a brief biography for press reference. Jakub's author work has been recognized internationally by newspapers such as the New Pelican, Coconut Creek News, Betterauds, Jukebox Mind, the Binghamton Herald, the New Edge Times, the Hollywood Tale, the bulletin of the Sun as well as Huffington Post's newsletter.
